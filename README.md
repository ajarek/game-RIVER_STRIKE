## How It Works
# River Strike is a retro-style vertically scrolling combat game inspired by the classic River Raid. Here's what's under the hood:

Core Mechanics
Procedural river generation: The river meanders dynamically using layered sine waves, creating a natural-feeling waterway that shifts with each level. Width varies, and difficulty increases as the river narrows and curves more aggressively.
Fuel system: Your fuel constantly drains. Stray over land and you crash; grab green fuel canisters floating in the river to refuel. A flashing warning appears when fuel is critically low.
Dual weapons: Bullets (Space/Z) for air-to-air and strafing runs, Bombs (B/X) that drop with gravity and deal massive damage — especially effective against bridges.
Enemies & Targets
Jets — fast, fragile, shoot back
Prop planes — slower, easier to hit
Helicopters — hover and weave, take 2 hits, rapid fire
Destroyers — armored ships (3 HP) with heavy guns
Patrol boats — fast, fragile river units
Fuel tanks — ground targets on the riverbanks that explode in satisfying chain blasts
Bridges — span the river, block your path, must be destroyed to pass safely (4 HP, bombs obliterate them)
Visual Polish
-Particle-based explosions with multi-color bursts
-Screen shake on damage, color flash effects
-Animated thrust flames, spinning helicopter rotors, ship smoke
-Water shimmer effect on the river surface
-Score popups float up from destroyed targets
-Damage visualization on bridges (cracks + darkening)
# Progression
-Kill targets to fill the level progress bar; advancing levels increase river speed, enemy spawn rate, and difficulty
-Bombs replenish slowly and bonus bombs are awarded on level-up
-The river gets wilder and enemies more aggressive with each level
